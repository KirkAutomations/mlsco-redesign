FAQ

Common Questions Answered

What services do you offer at MLS&CO?

We provide 13 services across Accounting & Auditing, Taxes, and Consulting, including bookkeeping, audits, R&D credits, tax planning, and more.

How do I qualify for R&D tax credits?

If your company develops new or improved processes, products, software, or techniques, you likely qualify. We conduct studies to maximize your claim.

What is a cost segregation study?

An engineering-based tax strategy that accelerates depreciation on building components for properties over $500K acquired after 1987.

Do you perform business valuations?

Yes, for buy-sell agreements, loans, ESOPs, and strategic decisions - not just sales.

What is BOI reporting?

Beneficial Ownership Information reporting required for most companies under the Corporate Transparency Act. Action required by May 2024 for existing entities.
